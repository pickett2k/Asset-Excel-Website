Asset Survey - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025
Summary: Asset Survey respects your privacy. All your data stays on your device unless you choose to export it. We don't collect any personal information or track your usage.

1. Information We Collect

Asset Survey collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Asset information you import from Excel/CSV files
  • Photos you take within the application for asset documentation
  • Location hierarchy data you create (sites, buildings, floors, rooms)
  • App settings and preferences you configure

2. How We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally on your device in the app's private directory
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not automatically sync or backup your data to any external servers
  • Your Control: You have complete control over your data and when it leaves your device
  • No Account Required: Asset Survey works without creating any user accounts

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the asset management functionality within the app:

  • Display your assets and locations within the app interface
  • Enable photo documentation and viewing
  • Generate export files when you choose to export data
  • Remember your app settings and preferences

4. Data Sharing

  • No Automatic Sharing: Asset Survey does not automatically share your data with any third parties
  • Export Only When You Choose: Data only leaves your device when you actively use the export function
  • Your Sharing Control: Exported data can be shared via your device's sharing capabilities (email, cloud storage, etc.) - this is entirely under your control
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data

5. Permissions We Request

Camera Access

  • Purpose: Used solely for taking photos of assets for documentation
  • Control: You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings
  • Storage: Photos are stored locally in the app's private directory

Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: Used only for selecting existing photos from your library to add to assets
  • Scope: Limited access - we only access photos you specifically select
  • No Browsing: We cannot browse or access your entire photo library

6. Data Security

  • Data is stored in the app's secure, private directory on your device
  • Photos are stored with secure file permissions
  • No network transmission occurs without your explicit action
  • App data is protected by your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

7. Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • You can clear all data at any time through the app's settings
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device
  • We do not retain any copies of your data on external servers

8. Children's Privacy

Asset Survey is designed for professional use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Users

Asset Survey is developed by Steven Williams, based in the United Kingdom. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, there are no international data transfers involved in normal app operation.

11. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or update any information
  • Delete: You can delete individual items or clear all data
  • Export: You can export your data at any time
  • Portability: Exported data is in standard formats (Excel, CSV, ZIP)
